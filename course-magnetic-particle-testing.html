NDT & Inspection

Magnetic Particle Testing (MT/MPT)

About Course

Magnetic Particle Testing is a non-destructive testing method used to detect surface and near-surface discontinuities in ferromagnetic materials. The course gives learners practical insight into magnetization, particle application, indication interpretation, and inspection reporting.

It is especially valuable in weld inspection, component maintenance, and manufacturing quality systems where ferromagnetic parts are routinely checked for fatigue cracks and flaws.

What is this Course?

It is a specialized inspection process for locating discontinuities in magnetic materials using magnetic fields and fine particles.

Eligibility

Suitable for technicians, welders, QA professionals, and engineering learners with an interest in inspection methods.

Course Duration

4 to 8 weeks with practical demonstration and evaluation.

Certification

Institutional certification with practical training and procedural awareness.
