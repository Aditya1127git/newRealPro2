This is for the first time in the country where Adarsh Management Institute of India is offering online Certifications in various IT and management fields via its division IILP (Industrial Integrated Learning Programs). Under this program working professionals get freedom of learning while working full time at corporate/industry they are associated with, the student can study over portal and needs to be on study center for only 16 hours in a semester for attending class, lab, workshop, seminar and pursuing examination.

The courses offered are designed considering UGC / AICTE norms, the university constitution and industry needs. The course is designed under supervision of Intellectuals from Education field and Corporate Leaders, this is for the first time where university has corporate advisory board panel to address the queries of students undergoing various courses.
