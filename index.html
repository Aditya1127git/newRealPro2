Vrindavan Technological University is situated in a picturesque, lush green area along the newly developed Jharkhand – Garhwa bypass road, approximately 10 kilometers from Garhwa railway station. This university boasts an expansive campus and is renowned for possessing one of the most impressive infrastructures in the state

Within the university, department heads and faculty members are distinguished by their extensive experience and unwavering commitment to education. Their passion is dedicated to imparting knowledge and fulfilling the mission of equipping students with exceptional career prospects and strong values, ultimately molding them into responsible citizens.

The institution was established in 2000 under the auspices of the Central India Institute of Technology. Since its inception, a steadfast dedication to excellence in teaching and research has positioned the group as a trailblazer and an inspiration for other educational institutions. Its proud academic heritage consistently draws the most gifted students who go on to make significant contributions to society.
