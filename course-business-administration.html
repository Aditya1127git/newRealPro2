Business Administration

About Course

Business Administration develops the strategic, organizational, and communication capabilities needed to lead teams, manage operations, and respond to changing market conditions. The course focuses on financial awareness, planning, people management, and decision-making.

It is ideal for learners who want to move into managerial roles and strengthen their ability to drive growth, performance, and sustainable business practice.

Eligibility

Open to students and professionals seeking leadership roles in business and service industries.

Course Duration

3 to 6 months depending on the academic and professional pathway.

Certification

Certification support with business communication and leadership modules.

Career Opportunities

Operations Manager, Executive Assistant, Business Analyst, Team Lead.

Industries

Retail, Banking, Healthcare, Logistics, Consulting, Startups.
