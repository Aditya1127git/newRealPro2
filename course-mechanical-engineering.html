Engineering & Technology

Mechanical Engineering

About Course

Mechanical Engineering focuses on the design, analysis, and maintenance of machines, systems, and industrial solutions that power modern infrastructure. The course builds a strong foundation in mechanics, materials, automation, and manufacturing technology.

With growing demand in production, energy, and industrial operations, this program prepares learners to contribute to reliable systems, efficient workflows, and innovation-driven engineering environments.

Eligibility

Open to students from science and diploma backgrounds who wish to pursue technical careers in engineering.

Course Duration

3 to 4 years depending on academic pathway and institutional structure.

Certification

Institutional certification and professional development support aligned to engineering practice.

Career Opportunities

Design Engineer, Production Engineer, Maintenance Engineer, Mechanical Technician.

Industries

Manufacturing, Automotive, Energy, Aerospace, Construction, Utilities.
