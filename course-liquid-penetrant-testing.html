NDT & Inspection

Liquid Penetrant Testing (PT/LPT)

About Course

Liquid Penetrant Testing is a surface inspection method used to reveal fine cracks, porosity, and discontinuities that are invisible to the naked eye. This course provides learners with practical understanding of penetrant application, dwell time, remover use, developer response, and defect interpretation for safety-critical components.

It is widely used in fabrication, maintenance, and quality assurance environments where early surface defect detection improves reliability and reduces rework.

What is this Course?

It is a professional inspection technique focused on identifying visible surface defects using penetrant chemistry and controlled workflow procedures.

Eligibility

Open to diploma, engineering, and technical professionals seeking hands-on NDT experience.

Course Duration

4 to 6 weeks with practical demonstrations and assessment.
