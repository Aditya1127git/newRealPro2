ENGINEERING COURSES

Our Engineering Programs are designed to develop technical knowledge, innovation, and practical problem-solving skills. Students receive quality education through modern laboratories, experienced faculty, and industry-oriented learning to prepare them for successful careers in engineering and technology..

Eligibility

Candidates must have passed Class 12th with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM) from a recognized board. Admission is subject to university norms and eligibility criteria.

Available Engineering Courses

  • B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering (4 Years)
  • B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science (4 Years)
  • B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering (4 Years)
  • B.Tech in Civil Engineering (4 Years)
  • B.Tech in Electrical Engineering (4 Years)
  • B.Tech in Electronics & Communication Engineering (4 Years)

Admission Process

Students can apply online or visit the university admission office. Admission is granted based on eligibility criteria and document verification.

Duration

The Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) program is of 4 years duration divided into eight semesters.

Course Highlights

  • Experienced & Qualified Faculty
  • Modern Engineering Laboratories
  • Industry-Oriented Curriculum
  • Project-Based Learning
  • Technical Workshops & Seminars
  • Internship Opportunities
  • Innovation & Research Support

Career Opportunities

Engineering graduates can pursue careers as Software Engineers, Civil Engineers, Mechanical Engineers, Electrical Engineers, Electronics Engineers, Data Analysts, AI Engineers, Project Engineers, Technical Consultants, and Government Officers. Students may also continue higher studies such as M.Tech or MBA.

Required Documents

  • 10th Marksheet
  • 12th Marksheet
  • Aadhar Card
  • Passport Size Photographs
  • Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • Migration Certificate (if applicable)
