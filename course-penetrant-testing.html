NDT & Inspection

Penetrant Testing (PT)

About Course

Penetrant Testing is a highly effective surface inspection technique used to reveal cracks, laps, and discontinuities that may not be visible to the naked eye. It is widely applied in fabrication, maintenance, and quality assurance operations where surface integrity directly affects safety and durability.

Designed for learners seeking a practical entry into inspection science, this course combines theory with applied demonstration of penetrant application, dwell time, developer action, and defect interpretation. It is especially valuable in sectors that demand rigorous quality checks and reliable defect detection at the production stage.

Eligibility

Suitable for diploma holders, graduates, technicians, and professionals in maintenance or manufacturing.

Course Duration

6 to 8 weeks with practical modules and certification assessment.

Certification

Institutional certification with practical evaluation and professional training completion support.

Career Opportunities

Quality Inspector, NDT Assistant, Weld Inspection Trainee, Maintenance Support Engineer.

Industries

Fabrication, Aerospace, Railways, Oil & Gas, Heavy Engineering, Power.
