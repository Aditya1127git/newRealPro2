MANAGEMENT COURSES

Our Management Programs are designed to develop leadership qualities, business knowledge, communication skills, and decision-making abilities. The curriculum combines academic learning with practical exposure to prepare students for successful careers in business, entrepreneurship, and corporate management.

Available Management Courses

  • Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) – 3 Years
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA) – 2 Years
  • Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) – 3 Years
  • Master of Commerce (M.Com) – 2 Years
  • Diploma in Business Management – 1 Year
  • Diploma in Human Resource Management – 1 Year
  • Diploma in Marketing Management – 1 Year

Eligibility

Candidates must have passed Class 12th from a recognized board for undergraduate programs. For postgraduate programs such as MBA and M.Com, candidates must possess a relevant bachelor's degree from a recognized university.

Course Duration

Management programs range from 1 to 3 years for diploma and undergraduate courses, while postgraduate programs such as MBA and M.Com are generally completed in 2 years.

Course Highlights

  • Experienced Faculty Members
  • Industry-Oriented Curriculum
  • Business Case Studies
  • Personality Development Sessions
  • Leadership & Communication Training
  • Seminars and Workshops
  • Entrepreneurship Development

Career Opportunities

Management graduates can build careers as Business Executives, Marketing Managers, Human Resource Executives, Financial Analysts, Sales Managers, Operations Managers, Entrepreneurs, and Business Consultants. Students may also pursue higher education and professional certifications in management and finance.

Admission Process

Students can apply online or visit the university admission office. Admissions are granted based on eligibility criteria, document verification, and university guidelines.

Required Documents

  • 10th Marksheet
  • 12th Marksheet
  • Graduation Marksheet (For PG Programs)
  • Aadhar Card
  • Passport Size Photographs
  • Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • Migration Certificate (if applicable)
