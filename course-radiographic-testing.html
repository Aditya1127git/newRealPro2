NDT & Inspection

Radiographic Testing (RT)

About Course

Radiographic Testing uses penetrating radiation to reveal internal discontinuities in materials and welds. It is a high-value method for locating subsurface defects in components where structural reliability is critical and internal quality cannot be inspected visually.

This course equips learners with the scientific basis of imaging, radiation safety, exposure technique, film interpretation, and defect evaluation. It is particularly relevant for high-consequence industries where preventive inspection supports long-term asset integrity and regulatory compliance.

Eligibility

Ideal for engineering students, technicians, and professionals working in inspection, fabrication, or maintenance environments.

Course Duration

8 to 12 weeks including applied theory, practical exposure, and evaluation.

Certification

Professional certification support with practical assessment and industry-oriented reporting modules.

Career Opportunities

Radiographic Technician, NDT Inspector, Quality Control Specialist, Integrity Analyst.

Industries

Energy, Petrochemical, Civil Infrastructure, Aerospace, Shipbuilding, Manufacturing.
