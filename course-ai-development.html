About Course

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning are transforming the way organizations automate intelligence, analyze complex data, and optimize operations. This course introduces learners to practical AI concepts, supervised and unsupervised learning, model evaluation, and modern deployment strategies used in industry.

By blending theory with applied exercises, the program prepares participants to work with intelligent systems that support automation, forecasting, recommendation, and decision-making across business and technology sectors.
