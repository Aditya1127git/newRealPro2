NDT & Inspection

Visual Testing (VT)

About Course

Visual Testing is a foundational non-destructive testing method used to detect surface-level defects, weld discontinuities, corrosion, and assembly issues in industrial components. It is one of the most widely used inspection techniques because it is fast, economical, and highly effective for early-stage quality control.

The course is designed to provide learners with hands-on knowledge of inspection standards, defect recognition, lighting methods, documentation practices, and reporting techniques used across high-reliability sectors. In industries where safety and reliability are critical, visual inspection plays a crucial role in reducing failures and maintaining operational integrity.

Eligibility

Open to students, technicians, and working professionals with a secondary or diploma-level background in engineering, manufacturing, or maintenance.

Course Duration

3 to 6 months with practical training and assessment modules.

Certification

Industry-recognized certification support aligned with global inspection standards and institutional evaluation.

Career Opportunities
