NDT & Inspection

Ultrasonic Testing (UT)

About Course

Ultrasonic Testing is a high-precision inspection method for detecting internal flaws in metals, weldments, and structural components. The course covers sound wave transmission, flaw evaluation, calibration, and advanced reporting used in critical industry environments.

It is widely adopted where internal integrity, weld quality, and structural reliability must be verified without damaging the component.

What is this Course?

This training develops the ability to apply ultrasonic wave-based inspection techniques for internal defect detection and quality verification.

Eligibility

Best suited to engineering students, welders, and technical professionals seeking advanced inspection credentials.

Course Duration

6 to 8 weeks with laboratory-style practicals and evaluation.

Certification

Professional certification support with practical orientation and inspection documentation training.

Career Opportunities

UT Inspector, NDT Specialist, QA/QC Engineer, Asset Integrity Analyst.

Industries

Oil & Gas, Power, Aerospace, Railway, Pressure Vessels, Shipbuilding.
