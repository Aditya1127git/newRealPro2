COMPUTER COURSES

Our Computer Courses are designed to equip students with modern technical skills required in the IT industry. The programs combine theoretical knowledge with practical training to prepare students for successful careers in software development, networking, cybersecurity, data management, and other emerging technologies.

Available Computer Courses

  • Certificate in Computer Applications (CCA) - 6 Months
  • Diploma in Computer Applications (DCA) - 1 Year
  • Advanced Diploma in Computer Applications (ADCA) - 2 Years
  • Programming with C & C++ - 6 Months
  • Python Programming - 6 Months
  • Java Programming - 6 Months
  • Web Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) - 6 Months
  • Full Stack Web Development - 1 Year
  • Data Entry & Office Automation - 6 Months
  • Computer Hardware & Networking - 1 Year

ELigibility

Candidates should have passed Class 10th or 12th from a recognized board. Some advanced courses may require basic computer knowledge.

Duration

Course duration ranges from 6 months to 2 years depending on the selected program.

Course Highlights

  • Experienced Faculty Members
  • Modern Computer Laboratory
  • Practical Based Learning
  • Project Work
  • Industry-Oriented Curriculum
  • Career Guidance Support

Career Opportunities

Students completing these programs can work as Software Developers, Web Developers, Computer Operators, Data Entry Executives, Technical Support Engineers, Network Administrators, IT Assistants, and Office Executives. They can also pursue higher education in Computer Science and Information Technology.

Admission Process

Students can apply online or visit the university admission office. Admission is granted based on eligibility criteria and document verification.

Required Documents

  • 10th Marksheet
  • 12th Marksheet (if applicable)
  • Aadhar Card
  • Passport Size Photographs
  • Transfer Certificate (if required)
