DIPLOMA COURSE

Our diploma programs are designed to provide students with practical knowledge, technical skills, and industry-oriented training. These programs prepare students for higher education as well as employment opportunities in various engineering and technology fields.

Available Diploma Courses

  • Diploma in Civil Engineering (3 Years)
  • Diploma in Mechanical Engineering (3 Years)
  • Diploma in Electrical Engineering (3 Years)
  • Diploma in Computer Science Engineering (3 Years)
  • Diploma in Electronics Engineering (3 Years)
  • Diploma in Automobile Engineering (3 Years)

ELigibility

Candidates must have passed Class 10th from a registered board.Admission is sbject to university rules and seat availability.

Duration

The diploma program duration is generally 3 years consisting of six semesters.

Career Opportunities

After completing the diploma course, students can work as Junior Engineers, Technicians, Supervisors, Quality Inspectors or pursue higher studies through lateral entry into B.Tech programs.
